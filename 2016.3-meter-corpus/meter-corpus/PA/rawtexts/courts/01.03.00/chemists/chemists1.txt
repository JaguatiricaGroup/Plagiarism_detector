CHEMISTS ADMIT SUPPLYING DEFECTIVE BABY MEDICINE<
By Harriet Tolputt, PA News<
Two chemists today admitted supplying defective medicine for a baby who later
died.<
Lisa Taylor Lloyd, 27 and Ziad Kattab, 25, pleaded guilty to supplying
peppermint water ``not of the nature and quality specified on the description''
at Chester Crown Court.<
The hearing followed the death of three-week-old Matthew Young, who died last
May after he was given the medicine to cure wind from Boots The Chemist at
Hallwood Health Centre in Runcorn.<
Taylor Lloyd and Kattab denied an earlier charge of manslaughter, which has
now been dropped.<
