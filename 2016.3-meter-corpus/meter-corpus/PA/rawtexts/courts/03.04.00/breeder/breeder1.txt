DOG BREEDER DEATH TRIAL<
By Brian Farmer, PA News<
A former door-to-door salesman was today due to stand trial for the murders of
a pedigree dog breeder and her elderly mother.<
Kevin Cotterell, 33, of Pentney, Norfolk, is accused of stabbing to death
45-year-old Janice Sheridan and Connie Sheridan at the isolated home they shared
in Upwell, Norfolk, in January 1999.<
Janice Sheridan was due to show her champion whippet, Dylan, at Crufts two
months after she died. The dog attended her funeral service.<
Cotterell has denied murder. His trial at Norwich Crown Court is due tolast
for a week.<
