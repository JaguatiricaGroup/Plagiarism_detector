DOOR-TO-DOOR SALESMAN GETS LIFE FOR DOUBLE MURDER<
By Brian Farmer, PA News<
A former door-to-door salesman was today given two life sentences for the
brutal murders of a dog breeder and her elderly mother.<
Kevin Cotterell, 33, of Pentney, Norfolk, pleaded guilty to stabbing to death
45-year-old Janice Sheridan and Connie Sheridan, 79, at the isolated cottage
they shared in Upwell, Norfolk, in January 1999.<
Cotterell had denied the murders since being arrested in April last year, but
changed his plea at the last minute today as his trial was due to begin at
Norwich Crown Court.<
Detective Inspector Paul Chapman, who led the murder investigation, described
Cotterell as ``clearly very, very evil and very wicked''.<
