EX-SALESMAN ADMITS MURDERING DOG BREEDER AND HER MOTHER<
By Brian Farmer, PA News<
A former door-to-door salesman today admitted the murders of a pedigree dog
breeder and her elderly mother.<
Kevin Cotterell, 33, of Pentney, Norfolk, pleaded guilty to stabbing to death
45-year-old Janice Sheridan and Connie Sheridan at the isolated home they shared
in Upwell, Norfolk, in January 1999.<
Janice Sheridan was due to show her champion whippet, Dylan, at Crufts two
months after she died. The dog attended her funeral service.<
Cotterell was due to be sentenced at Norwich Crown Court later today.<
