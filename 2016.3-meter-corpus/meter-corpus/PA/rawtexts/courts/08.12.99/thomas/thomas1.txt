By Brian Farmer, PA News<

accused of the murder of Thomas Marshall.<
Kevan Roberts, 52, of Eccles, Norfolk, denies strangling 12-year-old Thomas,
of Happisburgh, Norfolk, in August 1997.<
Thomas, who would have been 15 yesterday, vanished after leaving home on his
bike. His body was found the next day 50 miles away at a picnic area near
Thetford, Norfolk.<
