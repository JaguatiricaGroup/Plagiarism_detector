POLICEMAN NOT GUILTY OF ASSAULT WITH GAS SPRAY<
By Sinead McIntyre, PA News<
A police officer was today found not guilty of assaulting three people,
including a female colleague, with CS gas spray while breaking up a fight.<
Andrew Bateson, 28, of Greater Manchester Police Force broke down in tears as
the verdict was returned at Manchester's Minshull Street Crown Court.<
It took the jury of 10 men and two women just half an hour to find him not
guilty on three charges of causing actual bodily harm to female officer Deborah
Davidson, 30, Mr Francis Walsh, 50, and Joanne Bagguley, 24, while breaking up
the disturbance in Bolton, Greater Manchester, in November 1997.<
