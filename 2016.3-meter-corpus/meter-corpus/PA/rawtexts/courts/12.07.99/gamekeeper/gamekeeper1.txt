GAMEKEEPER TO BE SENTENCED FOR BADGER SNARES<
By Maria Breslin, PA News<
A gamekeeper convicted of setting illegal snares to kill badgers is due in
court for sentencing today.<
John Drummond was last month found guilty of 46 charges of killing badgers and
using snares on Lord Cavendish's Holker Hall estate, in Cumbria.<
Drummond, 32, of Old Park Farmhouse, Grange-over-Sands, Kendal, was
responsible for game management on the estate at Cark-in-Cartmel where the Duke
of Edinburgh regularly shoots pheasant.<
During the hearing at Kendal Magistrates Court, where Drummond is due to be
sentenced, Stipendiary Magistrate Peter Ward heard how RSPCA inspectors found
three badgers, two dead and one alive _ on the 14,000-acre site.<
