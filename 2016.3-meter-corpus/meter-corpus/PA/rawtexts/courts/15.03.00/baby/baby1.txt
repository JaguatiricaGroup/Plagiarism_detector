MOTHER IN COURT ACCUSED OF BABY MURDER<
By Jane Merrick, PA News<
A 29-year-old woman appeared in court today charged with the murder of her
four-month-old daughter.<
Emma-Jade Dyson's body was found in the Leeds/Liverpool canal in Burnley,
Lancashire, on Tuesday four hours after she was reported missing from her pram
in the town centre.<
Her mother Robina (correct) Hashim, of Clare Street, Burnley, appeared before
the town's magistrates charged with her murder.<
She spoke once only to confirm her name and address and was remanded in
custody.<
The case was adjourned to Preston Crown Court for a date to be fixed.<
Reporting restrictions were not lifted.<
