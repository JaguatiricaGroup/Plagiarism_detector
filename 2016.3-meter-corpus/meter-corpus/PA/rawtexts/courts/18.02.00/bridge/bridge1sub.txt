YOUTHS CONVICTED OF BRIDGE KILLING<
By Gordon Darroch, PA News<
Four youths were today convicted of the killing of a ``promising'' A level
student who was beaten, robbed, kidnapped and left to drown in a river following
a night club brawl.<
Jonathan Coles, 18, was pushed into the River Ouse near Newport Pagnell,
Buckinghamshire, by three of the youths, despite protesting that he could not
swim and the fact that he was ``severely disabled'' after being stripped of his
glasses.<
A jury at Luton Crown Court took 11-and-a-half hours of deliberations to
convict Brian Alleyne, 21, and 20-year-old Dwayne Dawkins, both of North Seventh
Street, Milton Keynes, and Darren Matthews, 17, of Neath Hill, Milton Keynes, of
Jonathan's murder.<
Jason Canepe, 20, of Great Denton, Eaglestone, Milton Keynes, was cleared of
murder by the jury, but had pleaded guilty during the trial to manslaughter.<
