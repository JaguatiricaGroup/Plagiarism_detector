PENSIONER IN COURT ON CHILD ABUSE CHARGES<
By Phil Hazlewood, PA News<
An 82-year-old man was appearing in court today facing charges of indecent
assault against boys at a children's home.<
The pensioner was arrested and charged last week after a joint investigation
by West Midlands Police and Birmingham social services into allegations of abuse
at the Tennall Assessment Centre on the outskirts of the city.<
The charges relate to offences allegedly committed between 1979 and 1983.<
The man, who is not being named, was given bail until his appearance before
Birmingham magistrates today.<
