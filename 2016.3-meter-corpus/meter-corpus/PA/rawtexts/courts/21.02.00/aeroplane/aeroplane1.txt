BUSINESSMAN FACES TRIAL ON MID-AIR INDECENCY CHARGE<
A businessman accused of indulging in a passionate clinch with a stranger on a
transatlantic flight was sent for trial when he appeared in court today.<
David Machin, 40, of Woodland Road, Wakefield, West Yorkshire, appeared before
Manchester Magistrates charged with outraging public decency and being drunk on
board the American Airlines aircraft.<
Machin  was committed to Manchester Crown Court to appear on April 3.<
The charges follow an alleged incident in the Business Class section of a
flight from Dallas to Manchester in October last year.<
