GUSCOTT ARRIVES TO FACE ROAD RAGE TRIAL<
By Dave Higgens, PA News<
England rugby star Jeremy Guscott arrived at court today to face charges in
connection with an alleged road rage attack.<
Guscott, 34, stepped out of a Mercedes van outside Bristol Crown Court wearing
a light grey suit, light blue shirt and blue tie, accompanied by lawyers.<
He said nothing as he walked the few paces into the main entrance.<
Guscott is alleged to have attacked 47-year-old antiques dealer Ken Jones
after he got out of his Mercedes sports car in Bath City centre on March 24.<
He denies causing grievous bodily harm. The trial is expected to last five
days.<
Guscott, who lives in Bath, won 65 caps for England and scored 30 tries -
putting him second in the all-time list after Rory Underwood.<
He has also taken part in three British Lions tours, scoring a series-winning
dropped goal in Durban, South Africa, two years ago.<
