MAN CHARGED OVER THREATS TO KILL JACK STRAW<
By Peter Beal, PA News<
A man was appearing before magistrates today charged with making threats to
kill Home Secretary Jack Straw and another MP.<
The 47-year-old man was appearing at court in Blackburn, Lancashire.<
Lancashire Police said the man, who was arrested and bailed on Friday, was
from the area. The other MP who was allegedly subjected to threats has not been
named.<
Mr Straw is MP for Blackburn.<
