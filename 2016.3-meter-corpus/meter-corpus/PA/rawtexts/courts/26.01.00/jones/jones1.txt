SOCCER BOSS FACES FOUR MORE CHARGES<
By Mark Benattar, PA News<
Southampton FC manager Dave Jones today faced four more charges relating to
offences against children.<
The former Everton defender now faces a total of 17 charges in relation to his
time as a care worker at a Merseyside children's home.<
The father-of-four did not enter a plea at the 20-minute hearing at Liverpool
Crown Court but had four more charges added to the indictment.<
He previously faced six indecent assault charges, six of assault and a further
charge relating to a serious sexual offence on a boy aged 16 or under.<
The 43-year-old was given conditional bail. He must live at his home address
in Southampton and not approach any witnesses.<
The case was adjourned for a further plea and direction hearing at a later
date and a trial date was set for November 27.<
