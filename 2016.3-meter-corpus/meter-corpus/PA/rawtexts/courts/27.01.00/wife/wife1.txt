MURDER PLOT WIFE JAILED FOR THREE YEARS<
By Matthew Cooper, PA News<
An unhappy housewife who recruited a hitman to murder her husband with a
crowbar as he lay asleep in bed was today jailed for three years.<
Mother-of-two Sandra Jones, whose husband Clifford, 35, survived last
February's murder bid in Uttoxeter, Staffordshire, wept in the dock as Mr
Justice Kay described the plot as ``wicked in the extreme''.<
Her co-conspirator and friend, Julie Harvey, 34, was jailed for 18 months for
her part in the ``madcap'' scheme.<
The two women's would-be assassin, convicted killer Matthew Lee, 53, was
jailed for life at Stafford Crown Court for his part in what the judge said was
an ``astonishing'' plan.<
