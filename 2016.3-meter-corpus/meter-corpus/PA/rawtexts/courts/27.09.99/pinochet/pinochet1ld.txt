SPAIN LISTS 36 CHARGES AGAINST PINOCHET AS HEARING BEGINS<
By Jo Butler and John Deane, PA News<
General Augusto Pinochet today faced 36 charges of torture and conspiracy to
torture as formal extradition proceedings against him finally opened in London.<
Prosecutors on behalf of the Spanish Government detailed 35 separate
allegations of torture against the 83-year-old former Chilean dictator, dating
from December 1988 to December 1989.<
They also accused him of orchestrating a systematic campaign of terror during
the last 15 months of his 17-year regime.<
Details of the charges were made public for the first time as the hearing
opened amid stormy scenes, with rival protesters demonstrating outside Bow
Street Magistrates Court in central London.<
