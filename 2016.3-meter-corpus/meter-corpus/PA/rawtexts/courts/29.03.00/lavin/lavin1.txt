FOOTBALLER SENT FOR TRIAL<
By Robert Westhead, PA News<
Bristol City defender Gerard Lavin was today sent for trial at crown court for
allegedly injuring a spectator by kicking a ball into the crowd.<
Lavin, 26, of Standford Road, Winscombe, north Somerset, is accused of assault
causing actual bodily harm.<
He appeared today before magistrates in Reading over the incident during a
Nationwide Second Division match at the Madejski Stadium in the town on August 7
last year.<
Lavin was remanded on conditional bail to appear at Reading Crown Court on May
9 for a plea and directions hearing.<
