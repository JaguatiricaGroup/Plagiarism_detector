A policeman who called a colleague - the aunt of model Naomi Campbell - a 'flat-nosed monkey' was fined £500 yesterday for using racially aggravated insulting words and behaviour. Pc Peter Ferguson, 42, pressed down his nose at Pc Sonia Campbell, 36, and said she 'could have gone six rounds with Mike Tyson' at a police quiz night at Kensington, London, Tower Bridge magistrates heard.
