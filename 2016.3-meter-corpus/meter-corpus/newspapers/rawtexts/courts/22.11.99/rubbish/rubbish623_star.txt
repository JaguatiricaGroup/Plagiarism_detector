A TRAINEE solicitor stole documents from a legal firm whose clients included disgraced ex-Cabinet Minister Jonathan Aitken and Princess Diana's lover James Hewitt. 

Benjamin Pell, 35, disguised as a council rubbish collector took black plastic rubbish bags from outside the Harley Street offices of Harkavys at night. Police found up to 200,000 documents at his home in Hendon, North London, Blackfriars Crown Court heard yesterday. They included information revealing Aitken had 'personal and public difficulties'. Edward Brown, prosecuting, said it was 'highly likely' Pell intended to try to sell information to a publisher. Pell admitted theft and will be sentenced later.
