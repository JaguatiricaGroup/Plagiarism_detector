A TOP dancer who was reduced to a wheelchair-bound mute by an alleged medical blunder launched a claim for £8 million yesterday.

Kerstin Parkin's heart stopped as her first child was born. Husband Mark claims delays by medics lead to crippling brain damage. Yesterday film of the pretty Latin American dancer before and after the tragedy was played to the High Court. Mark, 40, from Bramley, Surrey, is suing Bromley Hospital NHS Trust. Kerstin, 31, can't talk, walk or care for herself.
