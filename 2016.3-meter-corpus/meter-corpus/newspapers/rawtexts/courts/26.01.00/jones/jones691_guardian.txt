Liverpool crown court yesterday set November 27 as the trial date for Southampton FC manager Dave Jones, who is charged with 13 child abuse offences. Jones, a father of four, faces six indecent assault charges, six of assault and a further charge relating to a serious sexual offence on a boy aged 16 or under. The 43-year-old former Everton defender was given conditional bail.

The case was adjourned for a further plea and directions hearing.
