A DAD of four was jailed for life yesterday after scientists finally linked him to a murder 14 years ago.

Duncan Jackson, 37, beat and strangled 26-year-old Avril Dunn - just 4 ft lO ins - in Luton, Beds, in 1985. Her body was found in a wood.

Reading Crown Court heard advances in DNA testing had proved that a semen sample from her clothes belonged to him.
